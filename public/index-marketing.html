Legal & Compliance
Operating Within the Law
Innovate IQ LLC is structured to lawfully facilitate staffing and contract placements. Here's how our operations stay compliant.
🏛️

LLC Legal Structure

Operating as a Limited Liability Company gives Innovate IQ LLC the legal standing to enter into business contracts, engage clients, and pay contractors — all as a separate legal entity from its owner(s).

📄

Master Services Agreement (MSA)

Every engagement is governed by a written MSA or Independent Contractor Agreement (ICA). These define scope, deliverables, payment terms, and IP ownership — protecting both parties.

🧾

1099-NEC Reporting

Contractors paid $600 or more in a calendar year will receive a Form 1099-NEC as required by the IRS. Contractors are responsible for their own self-employment taxes under federal and state law.

⚠️

Worker Classification (IRS ABC Test)

Innovate IQ follows IRS guidelines to avoid worker misclassification. Contractors must demonstrate independent business operations. We do not guarantee W-2 status where 1099 applies.

🏢

Corp-to-Corp (C2C) Legality

C2C arrangements are 100% legal. If you have your own business entity (LLC, S-Corp, etc.), you can invoice Innovate IQ LLC directly under a B2B services agreement.

📊

Tax Treatment

Innovate IQ LLC is taxed as a pass-through entity by default — income and expenses flow to the owner's personal tax return. A CPA can advise on electing S-Corp tax treatment as the business grows.

🔒

Data Privacy

Personal data submitted (resume, contact info, SSN for tax purposes) is handled securely and used only for placement and tax reporting purposes. We do not sell your information to third parties.

Disclaimer: The information on this website is provided for general informational purposes only and does not constitute legal or tax advice. Laws governing independent contractors, LLC operations, and worker classification vary by state and may change. Innovate IQ Consulting LLC strongly recommends consulting a licensed employment attorney and a CPA before entering into any contractor or staffing arrangement. Nothing on this site creates an attorney-client relationship.
